Gaida M62 WEB TV 👋

Sannu da zuwa M62 WEB TV - Tashar talabijin ta yanar gizo da aka yi domin sanar da labarin Niger zuwa duniya. Mun yi aiki don sanar da kowane mutum da sautin jama'a, wasanni, shirye-shirye, wakoki, da kuma ilimi.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Sanar da labarin Niger zuwa duniya da aminci, tattalin arzikin gari da jin karuwar kowa.

Vision: Zama manyan tashar talabijin da aka gida a Niger, mai karbuwar kowa duk a gida da kaje gida.

Abubuwa Muke yi

  • 📰 Sanar da labarin Niger kowane rana
  • 🎬 Shirye-shirye na sinima da talabiji
  • ⚽ Sakamakon wasanni da takaitai
  • 🎵 Fitattun wakoki da muziki Niger
  • 📚 Karatu da ilimi para jama'a
  • 🎙️ Sautin jama'a da abubuwan haska
  • 📡 Live streaming kowane rana
  • 💬 Forum da tattaunawa tsakanin jama'a

Ai Tare da Mu? 🤝

Muke bukatarku don sanar da labarin Niger da kuma ga sautin jama'a. Idan kuna da jiya, kakaita, ko kuma sanin labari, tabbatai mu karantawa. Jiya da kuma abubuwa na jama'a sun kai mu.
